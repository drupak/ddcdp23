Our Partners

SAMA

The Saudi Central Bank, previously known as the Saudi Arabian Monetary Authority, established in 1952, is the central bank of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. SAMA is responsible for maintaining monetary and financial stability in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, in addition to supporting balanced economic growth.

TAHALUF

Tahaluf is bringing world-class events to Saudi Arabia and helping contribute to the Kingdom becoming a global events hub. Tahaluf will bring together vibrant communities from global industries through extraordinary in-person events and digital platforms. Tahaluf is a strategic joint venture between Informa PLC, SAFCSP and EIF

FINTECH SAUDI

Fintech Saudi was launched by the Saudi Central Bank in partnership with the Capital Market Authority in April 2018 to act as a catalyst for the development of the financial services technology (fintech) industry in Saudi Arabia. Its ambition is to transform Saudi Arabia into an innovative fintech hub with a thriving and responsible fintech ecosystem.

CMA

Capital Market Authority (CMA) regulates and develops the Saudi Arabian Capital Market by issuing required rules and regulations for implementing the provisions of Capital Market Law. The basic objectives are to create an appropriate investment environment, boost confidence and reinforce transparency.
